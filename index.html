What their noises mean

We all love our furry friends! But have you ever wondered what their noises and what their body language means? We did too. So we did some research for you to find out some clues. It turns out that their body language (and squeaks) can say a lot about what the guinea pig is feeling. Sometimes they can be hungry, they can be territorial, or they can be super happy--so happy that they literally jump of happiness!

Squeak, squeak, squeak!!
